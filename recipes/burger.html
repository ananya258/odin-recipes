Burger

Description

Burger is an American dish.It is made of bread,lettuce,meat and other veggies

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • hamburger buns
  • Onion
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Tomatoes
  • cucumber
  • Herbs
  • spices
  • Cheese Slice
  • pickles and sauces

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, mix ground meat with salt and pepper. Form into 4 patties.
  3. Grill or pan-fry patties for about 3â€“4 minutes per side, or until cooked to your liking.
  4. Add cheese slices in the last minute of cooking to melt.
  5. Toast the buns lightly on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Assemble burgers with lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and sauces as desired.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
