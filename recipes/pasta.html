Lasagna

Description

Pasta is an italian dish. It is made of cheese, bread,pasta and herbs

Ingredients

  • 200g (7 oz) pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant (about 30 seconds).
  3. Pour in crushed tomatoes. Stir in oregano, salt, and pepper. Let simmer for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss until well combined.
  5. Serve hot, topped with fresh herbs and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
