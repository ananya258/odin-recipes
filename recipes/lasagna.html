Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an italian dish. It is made of cheese, bread,pasta and herbs

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Noodles
  • Herbs
  • Sugar
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook meat, onion, and garlic in a skillet until browned.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer the sauce.
  4. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  5. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake covered for 25 minutes, then uncovered for 10 minutes.
  7. Let it cool before serving.
